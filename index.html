01 — What I Built

A contract intelligence system, proven on Brickyard.

CIVILIGN is a contract intelligence system. It ingests every document on a project — every letter, every spec section, every clause, every meeting minute — and builds a knowledge base that connects them all.

When a contractor files a claim, CIVILIGN finds the relevant contract provisions, reconstructs the correspondence trail, identifies precedent, and equips your team to build a defensible response — with citations to specific contract sections, not just page numbers.

Here’s how it supports the team:

  • Cited determination letters — your team builds responses grounded in specific contract sections. Every provision surfaced, every citation verified. Ready for review and agency letterhead.
  • Research matrices — every assertion mapped to provisions, correspondence, and project records. Your analysts see the complete picture before they write a word.
  • Executive briefings — exposure analysis and recommended positions assembled from the full record. Your leadership gets decision-ready material.
  • Hearing preparation — chronologies and contract citations for DRBs. Your team presents the complete defensible position.

The product is the defended claim, not the technology. CIVILIGN equips your team to get there faster — with the complete record at their fingertips.
